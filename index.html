Principal Investigator

Professor Li Yan graduated from Sun Yat-sen University with a bachelor's degree in 2008 and received her Ph.D. from the University of Hong Kong in 2012, under the supervision of Professor Xin-Yuan Guan. From 2013 to 2016, she worked as a postdoctoral fellow at the Li Ka Shing Faculty of Medicine, University of Hong Kong. From 2016 to 2024, she served as an Assistant Professor at the School of Life Sciences, Southern University of Science and Technology. Starting from August 2024, she joined the Department of Gastroenterology at Shenzhen Hospital of Southern Medical University.

Research Areas

1. Identification of genes associated with the development of hepatocellular carcinoma, esophageal cancer, and colorectal cancer.

2. Investigation into the roles of epitranscriptomic regulations, such as RNA editing and alternative splicing, in tumor initiation and progression.

3. Elucidation of regulatory mechanisms governing cancer stem cells and the tumor microenvironment (TME).

4. Exploration of molecular mechanisms underlying liver-gut axis interactions.
